# Design System Inspired by HubSpot

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

HubSpot is growth marketing made human. White canvas with coral-orange (#ff7a59) brand energy creates an approachable, results-focused aesthetic. The design bridges B2B utility (pipeline dashboards, email builders, analytics) with friendly, approachable visual language. Flywheel diagrams, workflow automation chains, and CTA-heavy layouts are signature.

**Key Characteristics:**
- White (#ffffff) canvas - clean, action-oriented
- HubSpot Orange (#ff7a59) as primary brand and CTA
- Growth marketing patterns: conversion funnels, email campaigns, analytics
- Friendly Inter font - weight 400-700
- CTA-heavy layouts - every section drives action
- Workflow builder: connected action nodes

## 4. Component Stylings

**Primary CTA:** #ff7a59 bg, #ffffff text, 8px radius, 12px 24px, 16px/600
Hover: #e66e50

**Secondary:** #f5f8fa bg, 1px #cbd6e2 border, #2d3e50 text

**Pipeline Stage Card:**
- White bg, 1px #cbd6e2 border, 8px radius
- Stage name (14px/700 uppercase) + deal count + total value
- Drag-handle on left | Deal cards as children

**Email Campaign Card:**
- White bg, 1px #cbd6e2 border, 8px radius
- Email thumbnail | Subject line 16px/600 | Open rate / Click rate metrics
- Status badge: Sent (green) / Draft (gray) / Scheduled (blue)

**Flywheel Diagram:**
- Circular flow: Attract > Engage > Delight
- Each segment: orange/blue/green with connecting arrows

**Navigation:**
- White bg, 1px #cbd6e2 bottom, 64px height
- HubSpot sprocket logo left | Main nav center | Upgrade CTA right (orange)

## 5. Layout
Max-width: 1200px | Section padding: 80px
Dashboard: sidebar 220px + fluid main | Card grids: 3-up

## 6. Key Rules
DO: Orange for primary CTAs | Flywheel/funnel diagrams | CTA after every section
DO NOT: Dark backgrounds | Multiple accent colors | Heavy data density without context
