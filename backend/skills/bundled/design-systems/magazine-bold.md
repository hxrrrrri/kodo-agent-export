# Design System: Magazine Bold (Abstract Theme)

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Magazine Bold is an abstract editorial design theme inspired by print magazines like Monocle, The New York Times Magazine, and Vogue. Warm cream canvas (#f8f1e8), authoritative serif display (Playfair Display / Freight), oversized typography, and print-inspired hierarchy. The design communicates: this content is worth your time.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Warm cream canvas (#f8f1e8) - paper-warm, editorial feel
- Serif display face (Playfair Display, Freight, or Tiempos) at large scale
- Oversized typography as primary design element (80-120px headlines)
- Pull quotes, bylines, issue numbers - editorial vocabulary
- Single decisive accent color: warm red (#d13f22) or ink blue (#1a3a5c)
- Visible column structure and dividers

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

**Surface:** Canvas #f8f1e8 | Page #fffaf2 | Card #f0e8d8 | Dark Band #1a1510
**Text:** Ink #111111 | Body #2a2520 | Muted #6a5b4f | Light Text #fffaf2
**Accent:** Warm Red #d13f22 (issue accent) | Ink Blue #1a3a5c (alternative)
**Border:** #c8b89a (warm rule lines) | #e0d0ba (subtle dividers)

## 3. Typography

**Display:** Playfair Display, Freight Display, Tiempos Headline - fallback: Georgia, serif
**Body:** Inter, Helvetica Neue, sans-serif (for UI/captions) | OR Georgia for longform body
**Mono:** SFMono-Regular, monospace

## 4. Component Stylings

**Feature Card:**
- Full-bleed image top | Warm cream bg below
- Category: 12px uppercase #d13f22 letter-spacing 0.12em
- Headline: Playfair 32px/700 #111111 | Standfirst: 16px/400 #2a2520
- Byline: 13px/600 uppercase #6a5b4f | Date: 13px muted
- 1px #c8b89a bottom rule

**Navigation (Editorial):**
- White or cream bg | Logo: bold serif wordmark | Issue number right
- Thin 2px black top stripe (issue indicator accent)
- Nav: sans-serif 14px/600 uppercase #111111 letter-spacing 0.08em

**Pull Quote:**
- Large serif italic 28px/400 #111111 | Left: 4px solid #d13f22 border
- Generous padding | Attribution: 14px/600 #6a5b4f

**Issue Cover Block:**
- Full-bleed photography | Overprinted headline at 80-120px
- Issue number badge top-left: #d13f22 bg, #ffffff text

**Article Layout:**
- Two columns at desktop (text + sidebar) or single column
- Drop cap on first paragraph | Pull quotes interrupt text

## 5. Layout
Spacing: generous and editorial | Section padding: 80px
Max-width: 1100px | Text column: 680px max | Gutter: 40px
Grid: 12-column with editorial asymmetry

## 6. Elevation
No shadows | Depth from surface color contrast | Rules (1px lines) as structural element
Photography creates depth | Red accent creates focal points

## 7. Key Rules
DO: Serif headlines at extreme scale | Visible column structure | Print editorial vocabulary
DO NOT: Sans-serif headlines | Decorative gradients | Card-heavy layouts | Small type
