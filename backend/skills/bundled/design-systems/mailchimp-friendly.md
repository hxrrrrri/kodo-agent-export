# Design System Inspired by Mailchimp

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Mailchimp is email marketing made friendly. Signature yellow (#ffe01b) as the brand crown jewel - used in headers, hero sections, and the famous Freddie mascot's hat. Dark navy (#241c15) text creates warm contrast. The design strikes a balance between professional marketing platform and approachable small-business tool. Workflow builders, email campaign editors, and analytics charts are the primary components.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Signature yellow (#ffe01b) in headers and brand moments
- Dark navy (#241c15) ink for high warmth contrast
- Off-white/warm white (#fffef5) canvas for main content
- Friendly Inter/Graphik typography - weight 400-700
- Email campaign cards, audience segment charts, automation flows
- Chimp mascot moments in illustrations

## 4. Component Stylings

**Primary CTA:** #241c15 bg, #ffe01b text, 4px radius, 12px 24px, 15px/600
Hover: #ffe01b bg, #241c15 text

**Campaign Card:**
- White bg, 1px #d9d0c4 border, 8px radius, 16px padding
- Email preview thumbnail (16:9) | Status badge top-right
- Campaign name: 16px/700 #241c15 | Audience: 14px muted | Metrics: open rate + click rate
- Date + action buttons footer

**Yellow Hero Band:**
- Full-width #ffe01b background | Dark navy text
- Heading: 40px/800 #241c15 | CTA: #241c15 bg button

**Navigation:**
- #241c15 bg sidebar | Freddie logo top | Yellow active state
- Nav items: 14px/500 light text | Active: #ffe01b text + left border

**Automation Flow:**
- Node cards: white bg, 1px #d9d0c4 border, 8px radius
- Connected by gray lines | Email node, Wait node, Condition node
- Trigger: #ffe01b border | Action: #007c89 border

**Audience Chart:**
- Bar/donut charts | Primary: #241c15 | Secondary: #007c89 | Yellow: #ffe01b

## 5. Layout
Max-width: 1200px | Sidebar: 220px dark | Content: fluid white
Campaign grid: 3-col | Section padding: 64px

## 6. Key Rules
DO: Yellow in header bands and brand moments | Dark navy ink on yellow | Warm off-white canvas
DO NOT: Yellow on small buttons (use navy) | Multiple accent colors | Cold corporate palette
