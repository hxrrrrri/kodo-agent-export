Design System

Confident movement, reduced to black and white.

Uber pairs stark monochrome surfaces with pill-shaped controls, full-bleed city imagery, and direct typography built for scanning at speed.

Where to?

Component rules

Pill controls carry the interaction language. Cards stay white with whisper-soft shadows. Text uses true black on white and white on black for maximum legibility.

Pill-shaped actions

Primary buttons, chips, selectors, and navigation actions use 999px radius.

Monochrome hierarchy

Human contrast

Photography and illustrations add warmth against the engineered UI chrome.
